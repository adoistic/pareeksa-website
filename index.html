Examinations

The core of what we do

Four parts of one job: getting answers off paper or screen, marked fairly, and turned into results an institution can defend.

01

OMR software & services

Optical mark recognition for answer sheets, surveys and feedback forms. We supply the reading software and run the scans, from a few thousand sheets to a few hundred thousand. You get checked data and an audit trail for every sheet.

02

On-screen marking

Markers score scanned booklets on screen instead of on paper. The candidate's identity stays hidden, so marking stays fair, and a webcam check confirms the right person is doing it. Each institution runs its own instance, in its own name.

03

Online examination

Run a test on screen from start to finish: question banks, timed papers, randomised sets and live invigilation. Results are ready when the exam closes, not weeks later.

04

Pre & post examination

The work around the exam: registration, hall tickets, seating and attendance, scanning, tabulation, result processing. We take the parts you would rather not staff and hand back checked output.

Beyond the exam hall

Everything around it

The same care applied to documents, software and AI — for institutions and companies that need the work done right.

Document intelligence

05

OCR

Turn scanned pages and PDFs into text you can search and edit: printed forms, registers, books, ledgers. We tune the model to your documents instead of a generic one.

06

ICR

Read hand-printed characters from filled forms and application boxes. Every field comes back with a confidence score, so a person checks only what is doubtful.

07

Document scanning

High-volume scanning of files, registers and archives. We come to you or you ship to us; you get organised digital copies, named and indexed the way you work.

08

Document management

A place to store, find and control documents: version history, access by role, full-text search, retention dates. Built around your filing logic, not a stock template.

09

Printing

Print work tied to exams and records: question papers under controlled handling, OMR sheets, certificates, mark sheets, variable-data runs. Secure where it has to be.

Software, AI & language

10

Custom portals

Web and mobile apps built to fit your process, not bent around a framework you will fight later. Admin portals, candidate apps, dashboards. We build, then maintain.

11

AI modules

Specific AI dropped into a real task: search across messy archives, a research assistant, scripting that clears repetitive work. Scoped to the job, not a demo.

12

Agentic AI — voice calling

An AI that takes phone calls and holds a real conversation. It follows your flow, answers questions, and books or routes the outcome — and it sounds human.

13

App security testing

We test your app the way an attacker would and report what breaks, with steps to reproduce and a fix priority. Web, mobile, and the APIs behind them.

14

Translation

Documents and content moved between languages by people who read both, with the meaning kept intact. For when records or material cross a language line.

Why Pareeksa

Named for the work

Pareeksa is परीक्षा — Sanskrit for examination. The root, pari + iksh, means to see thoroughly, to look at a thing from every side. That is the job we set ourselves, whether the thing under examination is an answer booklet, a stack of records, or an app's defences.

The work is high-stakes; a student's result or an institution's record often rides on it. So we build for accuracy first, keep a record of every step, deploy on your terms, and stay involved after launch.
